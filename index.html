    What could you build?

    This is a little engine for your imagination. Tell it what you do, and it'll show you the offers, artifacts, and experiences you could create inside your own community. Then it helps you actually start.

    1
    Your Gifts
    Choose up to three of your main gifts. The things you carry and do, not who you help.
    2
    Build Your Plan
    Move through offers, artifacts, and experiences. Add what lights you up, skip what doesn't.
    3
    Your Plan
    Get your blueprint, a ready-to-use Claude prompt for each piece, and a checklist to keep you moving.
    One gentle rule before you start
    You're going to want to build everything. Don't. Pick your first three and build those well. A community grows from one thing done with love, not ten things half-started. The rest will keep.
